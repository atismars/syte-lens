Built for parents.

Syte.Lens doesn't just ask "is this a scam?" It asks "is this okay for my child?"

  • Age ratings: All Ages through 18+, with content flags for sexual content, violence, profanity, drugs, gambling, and scary content.
  • Stranger-contact risk: flags chat, DMs, livestreams, and unmoderated comments where adults can reach kids.
  • Money traps: loot boxes, "free Robux" scams, and in-app-purchase pressure aimed at children.
  • Child-privacy (COPPA): whether the site collects data from minors.

How it works

1

Visit any site

The toolbar icon turns green, amber, or red for sites we've already rated.

2

Click the icon

The side panel opens and analyzes the page with AI, only when you ask.

3

Get the verdict

A clear score, kids-safety rating, and the reasons behind it, in seconds.

🔒 Privacy by design

A full analysis runs only when you click. We never build a profile of your browsing, we have no accounts, and we never sell your data. Icon coloring uses an anonymous domain-only lookup. The whole client is open source, so you can read every line. Read the full privacy policy.
